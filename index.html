Georgia Cities

Atlanta

Population: Approximately 500,000 (2023 estimate)

Demographics: Diverse population with a mix of African American, Caucasian, Hispanic, and Asian residents.

Average Salary: Around $60,000 per year

Cost of Living: Moderate compared to other large cities

Weather: Humid subtropical climate with hot, humid summers and mild winters

Augusta

Population: Approximately 200,000 (2023 estimate)

Demographics: Diverse population with a notable African American community

Average Salary: Around $45,000 per year

Cost of Living: More affordable than Atlanta

Weather: Humid subtropical climate with hot summers and mild winters

Savannah

Population: Approximately 150,000 (2023 estimate)

Demographics: Largely African American population with a rich history of African American culture and the arts

Average Salary: Around $40,000 per year

Cost of Living: Lower than in cities like Atlanta

Weather: Humid subtropical climate with hot, humid summers and mild winters
